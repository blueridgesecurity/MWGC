The Business of Homes

MWGC was built around real homeowner needs — not just construction tasks. From renovation and restoration to building and maintaining homes, we understand how every decision affects the integrity and long-term value of your home.

Michael Walker has been in the business of homes for over two decades. He is licensed as a General Contractor, Electrician, Plumber, Real Estate Broker, and Auctioneer — a combination that provides clients with practical guidance grounded in both construction expertise and real-world property ownership.

Whether evaluating build potential, managing complex remodels, or navigating unexpected site challenges, Michael leads each project with steady experience, attention to detail, and a clear understanding of what’s at stake.
